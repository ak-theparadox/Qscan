🚀 Qscan

Qscan is a fast, lightweight, privacy-focused QR & barcode tool built for modern mobile users.

Scan, generate, and manage QR codes instantly — all directly in your browser with zero backend.

---

⚡ Features

🔍 QR & Barcode Scanner

- Real-time camera scanning
- Automatic back camera detection
- Flip camera support
- Scan from uploaded images
- Instant result popup with copy support
- Fully on-device (no data upload)

---

⚡ QR Code Generator

- Generate QR codes for text & URLs
- Instant QR rendering
- Download as PNG
- Clean and minimal interface

---

🧰 Tools (SaaS-style Dashboard)

- Centralized tools page
- Expandable architecture for multiple QR utilities
- Designed for future tools like:
  - 📶 WiFi QR Generator
  - 💰 UPI Payment QR (India-focused)
  - 💬 WhatsApp QR
  - 👤 Contact QR
  - 📸 Social Media QR

---

🔒 Privacy First

Qscan is built with a strict privacy-first approach:

- ❌ No data is uploaded to any server
- ✅ All processing happens locally in your browser
- 🔐 Your camera feed never leaves your device

---

📱 PWA Ready

- Installable as an app
- Works offline (cached assets)
- Fast loading even on low networks

---

🛠️ Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3 (Glassmorphism UI)
- Vanilla JavaScript
- "html5-qrcode" (https://github.com/mebjas/html5-qrcode)
- "qrcode.js" (https://github.com/soldair/node-qrcode)
