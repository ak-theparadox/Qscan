🚀 Qscan

Qscan is a fast, privacy-first QR & barcode toolkit that works entirely in your browser.

Scan, generate, and share QR codes instantly — with zero data upload.

- ⚡ Generator — create QR codes for text & URLs
- 🧰 Tools — WiFi, UPI, WhatsApp & more (expanding)
- 🔒 Privacy-first — everything runs locally
- 📱 PWA-ready — installable & works offline
